Add the library of Servo motor & Ultrasonic sensor

1] Open Arduino IDE 
2] Click on Sketch 
3] Click on Include Library
4] Click on Add.ZIP library..
5] Choose the files
	*Arduino_New_Ping-master
	*Servo-master
6] Open the ino file
	Obstrucle_Avoiding_Robot.ino
7] Compile it and upload to the arduino board
